About Us

Taking into consideration the importance of Industrial Visits during the learning years of college we have created a web application for Industrial Visit Management. It provides the user who may be a college coordinator or a college representative who manages the IV can login and have a look at the various tour packages available for their students. Packages are provided according to the subject or branch, number of people, number of days etc. Another users are the companies which can add their tour packages, each user has other functionalities too.

The project is the implementation of front-end development learned in Web Technology and back-end development in DBMS.
